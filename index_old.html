I am currently an associate professor in the school of Cyber Science and Engineering of Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I have obtained my PhD degree in March 2019 from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. During my PhD studies, I was honored and fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Shing-Chi CHEUNG in the CASTLE group. I also had an unforgettable visiting experience at UC, Davis under the supervision of Prof. Zhendong Su. My research interests mainly focus on program analysis, automated debugging and software security. My official Homepage at HUST is: http://faculty.hust.edu.cn/wenming.
